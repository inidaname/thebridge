About The Bridge

The Bridge is a Pan North Central Zone of Nigeria socio-cultural group made up of the good people of the Zone encompassing Benue State, Kogi State, Kwara State, Niger State, Nasarawa State, Plateau State and the F.C.T Abuja.

The focus is for the people of North Central States to firm up a relationship for national relevance, strength and value devoid of ethnic and religious undertones for the purpose of advancing our economic, political and sociocultural power using as *The Bridge* our geographical location, cultural affinities and contiguity with the remaining Nigeria's five geopolitical zones.

We understand our differences in tribes, religions and other cultural nuances. We also understand the power in our diversity. The essence of The Bridge is to harness the strengths in our individual groups for the purpose of uplifting the entire Zone.

At The Bridge, we believe that the strength of the entire Zone is in its individual components. Hence, we clasp our hands to demonstrate strength in unity as we walk into a cultural renaissance, political emancipation, economic progress, universal well-being and the general good of the people of the North-Central Zone in particular and Nigeria in general.

Our core values are anchored on inclusivity, transparency, togetherness, peace and progress.

True to our name and essence, at The Bridge we serve as the veritable linkage which espouses recognition and respect for the rights of individuals to align with different political platforms. So, irrespective of our political linings, we are united in our quest to better the lots of our people. The interest of the Zone is supreme above all other interests.

To this end, we invite all well-meaning people of the Zone and friends of the Zone to come on board this train.

Together, we can build more bridges across our differences.
